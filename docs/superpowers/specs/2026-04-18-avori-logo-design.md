# Avori Logo Design

## Goal

Create a primary logo system for `Avori` that feels current, bold, minimal, and TikTok-native without drifting into a soft 2010s "millennial startup" or "modern mom" aesthetic.

## Approved Direction

- Brand name: `Avori`
- Logo architecture: `wordmark + symbol`
- Wordmark: lowercase, bold sans-serif
- Symbol: compact rounded abstract mark that can stand alone
- Tone: bold, minimal, current, broad-appeal, younger-skewing
- Visual references: the overlap between `rhode`, `POP MART`, and `BAGSMART`
- Chosen visual lane: `A` from the bold-minimal exploration
- Rejected directions: serif wordmarks, brown beauty branding, tech-clean, fashion-clean, editorial boutique styling, overly soft beauty marks

## Design Requirements

### Wordmark

- Use a sans-serif construction only
- Keep the wordmark lowercase: `avori`
- Weight should feel heavy, confident, and immediate at small sizes
- Curves should be rounded and compact rather than delicate
- Spacing should be tight enough to feel branded, but not compressed
- The overall silhouette should feel more block-like than elegant

### Symbol

- The symbol should visually relate to the `A` in `Avori` without becoming a literal monogram
- Form should be simple enough to work at small sizes
- Corners and geometry should feel rounded, chunky, and compact
- The symbol must work independently for avatar, favicon, packaging stamp, and social profile use
- The symbol should read fast and feel bold before it feels luxurious

### Color

- Signature brand color: electric blue
- Primary blue: `#2563FF`
- Dark support: `#0F1B3D`
- Light support: `#EAF1FF`
- Backgrounds: white, very light blue, or transparent
- The system should remain usable in one color
- Black and white alternates should exist, but electric blue is the main identity color
- Avoid warm brown, beige, muted gray, and generic neutral-first branding

## Deliverables

- Primary horizontal lockup: symbol + wordmark
- Symbol-only mark
- Transparent SVG exports
- PNG exports for immediate use
- Files saved to `~/Desktop`

## Success Criteria

- Reads as a credible DTC brand rather than a generic dropshipping store
- Feels bolder, younger, and more current than the initial soft directions
- Works on TikTok profile surfaces and future DTC packaging
- Remains legible and recognizable in monochrome
- Has a memorable signature color rather than relying on safe neutrals

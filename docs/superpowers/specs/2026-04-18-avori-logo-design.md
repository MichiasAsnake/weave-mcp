# Avori Logo Design

## Goal

Create a primary logo system for `Avori` that feels current, premium, and TikTok-native without drifting into a soft 2010s "millennial startup" aesthetic.

## Approved Direction

- Brand name: `Avori`
- Logo architecture: `wordmark + symbol`
- Wordmark: lowercase, bold sans-serif
- Symbol: compact rounded abstract mark that can stand alone
- Tone: clean, bold, modern, premium, approachable
- Visual references: modern DTC beauty/lifestyle brands in the `rhode` / `Jolie` lane
- Rejected directions: serif wordmarks, overly soft beauty marks, editorial boutique styling

## Design Requirements

### Wordmark

- Use a sans-serif construction only
- Keep the wordmark lowercase: `avori`
- Weight should feel confident and current, not thin or delicate
- Curves should be smooth and slightly rounded rather than rigid or mechanical
- Spacing should be tight enough to feel branded, but not compressed

### Symbol

- The symbol should visually relate to the `A` in `Avori` without becoming a literal monogram
- Form should be simple enough to work at small sizes
- Corners and geometry should feel rounded and compact
- The symbol must work independently for avatar, favicon, packaging stamp, and social profile use

### Color

- Primary mark color: warm dark neutral in the espresso / deep brown family
- Backgrounds: off-white, cream, or transparent
- The system should remain usable in one color

## Deliverables

- Primary horizontal lockup: symbol + wordmark
- Symbol-only mark
- Transparent SVG exports
- PNG exports for immediate use
- Files saved to `~/Desktop`

## Success Criteria

- Reads as a credible DTC brand rather than a generic dropshipping store
- Feels bolder and more current than the initial soft directions
- Works on TikTok profile surfaces and future DTC packaging
- Remains legible and recognizable in monochrome
